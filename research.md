My research focuses on physical oceanography and climate variability.

## Eddy Kinetic Energy

## Ocean Heat Content

Variability of isopycnal ocean heat content in the Northeast Pacific.

## Ocean–Climate Interaction

Understanding how ocean circulation variability interacts with climate variability.
