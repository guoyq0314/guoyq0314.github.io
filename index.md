## About Me

I am an Associate Professor of Physical Oceanography at the College of Marine Science and Technology, [Zhejiang Ocean University](https://www.zjou.edu.cn), Zhoushan, China. I also serve as a Master's supervisor.

My research focuses on **large-scale ocean circulation variability**, **mesoscale eddy dynamics**, and **ocean–climate interactions**, with a particular emphasis on the North Pacific Ocean and its role in climate variability. I combine observational data (satellite altimetry, Argo floats, ship-based CTD), reanalysis products, and ocean model output to investigate how the ocean varies on interannual to decadal time scales.

I received my Ph.D. in Physical Oceanography from **Ocean University of China** (2019), my M.Sc. from the **First Institute of Oceanography, Ministry of Natural Resources** (2015), and my B.Sc. in Ocean Science from **Nanjing University of Information Science and Technology** (2012).

## Research Interests

- **Ocean Circulation Variability:** interannual-to-decadal variability of large-scale currents, subtropical mode water
- **Mesoscale Ocean Eddies:** statistical characteristics, three-dimensional structure, eddy kinetic energy variability
- **Isopycnal Ocean Heat Content:** variability and mechanisms in the subtropical and subpolar Pacific
- **Ocean–Climate Interaction:** ENSO dynamics, Central Pacific El Niño, precipitation teleconnections
