## About Me

I am an Associate Professor of Physical Oceanography at Marine Science and Technology College, [Zhejiang Ocean University](https://www.zjou.edu.cn), Zhoushan, China. I also serve as a Master's supervisor.

My research focuses on **large-scale ocean circulation**, **ocean mesoscale eddies**, and **climate change**, with a particular emphasis on the North Pacific, El Niño-Southern Oscillation, and Pacific Decadal Variability. I combine observational data, reanalysis products, and ocean model output to investigate how the ocean varies on interannual to decadal time scales.

I received my Ph.D. in Physical Oceanography from **Ocean University of China** (2019), my M.Sc. from the **First Institute of Oceanography, Ministry of Natural Resources** (2015), and my B.Sc. in Ocean Science from **Nanjing University of Information Science and Technology** (2012).

## Research Interests

- **Ocean Circulation:** interannual-to-decadal variability of large-scale currents, subtropical mode water
- **Mesoscale Eddies:** statistical characteristics and three-dimensional structures of eddies, eddy kinetic energy variability
- **Ocean Heat Content:** variability and trend of ocean heat content, isopycnal ocean heat content
- **Climate Variability:** Central Pacific El Niño
